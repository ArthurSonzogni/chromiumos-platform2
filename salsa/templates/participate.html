This experiment consists of {{ treatments|length }} treatments. Each treatment is a variation on the touchpad settings. To participate in this experiment, you will try out each of these treatments and then provide us with feedback regarding their performance.

To try out an experiment, simply open up Crosh with Ctrl + Alt + t and paste in the given command.
